Space Rock Defense by Micah Welch.
-------------------------------------------------------

KEYBOARD CONTROLS:
   W 		- Move Forward
   A		- Turn Left
   D		- Turn Right
   S		- Slow Down
   SPACE	- Fire Laser
   Q		- Shield

   +		- Add an asteroid
   -		- Remove an asteroid

NOTES:
   - Laser damage is based on how much energy you have.
   - Hitting an asteroid without your sheild drains your armor.
   - Hitting an asteroid with your shield on, drains energy.
   - Red rings around asteroids represent their health.
   - Laser will pierce asteroid if it's damage is more than the asteroid had.
